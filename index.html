Our Services

At Omire-Wig-Work, We Offer Specialized Wig Services Only.

Our Services Include:

Wig Ventilating (Hand-Ventilated Wigs)

Carefully hand-knotting individual hairs strands onto lace to create a natural-looking wig with proper density, airflow, and a realistic finish.

Custom Wig Making

Personalized wig creation to meet your specific style and fit requirements.

Wig Reconstruction & Repairs

Restoring damaged or worn wigs to their original condition with high-quality materials and techniques.

Frontal & Closure Ventilating

Professional ventilating services for frontals and closures to ensure comfort and a natural look.

Wig Maintenance & Finishing

Deep cleaning and touch-up services to maintain your wig's appearance and longevity, and endure long lasting use.

Every service is handled with care, hygiene, and professional standards.
